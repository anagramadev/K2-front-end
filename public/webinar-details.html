Introduction to flat roofs

Beginner

60 min

08.06.2022, 10:30-11:30 CET

Register Full schedule
Flat Roof

In this one-hour Webinar we present our mounting systems for trapezoidal sheet metal roofing with fixation directly in the roof cladding or sheet metal and introduce you to planning with K2 Base.

  • Presentation of the mounting systems and technical differences
  • Long rails, short rails, floating installation and optional elevation
  • K2 Base: Roof surfaces, module arrays and blocks, portrait vs. landscape
